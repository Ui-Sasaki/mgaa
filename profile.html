INTRO TO Mrs. GREEN APPLE

Mrs. GREEN APPLE is a Japanese rock band formed in 2013. Known for their unique blend of pop and rock, they made their major debut in July 8th, 2015 with EMI Records. Over the years, they've captivated audiences with their energetic performances and heartfelt lyrics.

History of the Group

On July 8th, 2015, Mrs. Green Apple officially debuted after releasing their mini-album [Variety].
From 2015~2022 they were a band group consisting of 5 members: Motoki Ohmori (vo./gt.), Wakai Hiloto(gt.), Ryoka Fujisawa(piano/flute), Ayaka Yamanaka(drums), and Takano Kiyokazu(bass).
Yet, due to multiple issues combined with Ayaka and Takano wanting to do something else in life, they decided to announce a hiatus on July 8th, 2020 that lasted a year and 8 months until March of 2022 starting their "Phase 2", changing the number of members from 5 to 3 as we all know now.

Members

Motoki Ohmori

Position: Vocals & Guitar

Born: September 14, 1996

Motoki Ohmori (大森元貴) is the voice, heart, and creative genius behind Mrs. GREEN APPLE. Born on September 14, 1996, he’s been writing songs since he was a teenager and founded the band while still in high school. With his soaring vocals, powerful stage presence, and poetic lyricism, Motoki quickly captured the hearts of fans across Japan (and beyond!). He’s not just a frontman — he writes, composes, and leads the vision of the band. In 2021, while the band was on hiatus, Motoki launched a solo project that let him explore deeper and more experimental sounds. Whether he’s rocking out or pouring his heart into a ballad, there’s no denying: he's special.

Hiroto Wakai

Position: Guitar

Born: October 8, 1996

Hiroto Wakai is the guitarist and co-founder of Mrs. GREEN APPLE. Known for his sharp playing and bright energy on stage, he brings the rock edge to MGA’s sound. Beyond the band, he's also a fashion icon among fans, often showing up in bold, stylish fits. Being a childhood friend of motoki, they've had a special bond.

Ryoka Fujisawa

Position: Keyboard

Born: May 19, 1993

Ryoka is the keyboardist and sonic architect behind many of Mrs. GREEN APPLE’s lush melodies. His musicality adds emotional depth and elegance to their sound. Quiet off stage but powerful in performance, he's the group’s calm presence and hidden MVP. He can also play the flute (having won #1 in a Japan flute contest) and was invited to join Mrs. Green Apple by Motoki the moment they met due to his fun and exciting aura.
